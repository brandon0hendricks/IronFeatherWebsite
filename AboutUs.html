OUR STORY

IronFeather Games started in 2024 as a project between two friends who wanted to transition from playing games to building them. What began as a series of "what if" conversations eventually turned into a dedicated studio focused on creating the kinds of experiences we felt were missing from our own libraries.

We’ve always been drawn to the work of indie pioneers like The Behemoth, Chucklefish, and Team Cherry. Their approach to game design is a major influence on us—not just in the mechanics we build, but in the belief that a small, focused team can create something just as impactful as a larger studio.
